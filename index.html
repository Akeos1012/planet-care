ℹ️ About PlanetCare

🌍 PlanetCare is an interactive educational platform that brings climate-change learning to life through engaging mini-games and decision-making scenarios.

👨‍🏫 Teachers control sessions in real-time, send engaging questions, and track student performance on live leaderboards.

🎮 Students join via unique PIN codes and compete in low-poly portrait-style gameplay with immediate feedback.

Version 1.0 • Built with Vue.js + Firebase
